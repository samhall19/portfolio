About Me

I’m a game designer with 10 years of experience building commercial VR and PC titles. I work primarily as a generalist, with a strong focus on gameplay systems, level design, and technical implementation. I enjoy taking ideas from early concept through to polished, shippable features, using hands-on scripting and engine tools to prototype, iterate, and refine designs quickly. I’ve led design on multiplayer systems and have extensive experience working within agile, cross-disciplinary teams. I’m particularly interested in finding unique gameplay mechanics, and I enjoy collaborating closely with programmers and artists to solve complex design problems
